# Clear Automations — Design Context

## Target Audience
**SMB owners and operations managers** (10-100 employees, $1-20M revenue) who are losing leads because their follow-up is too slow, their processes are manual, and they don't have the tech team to fix it. They find Clear Automations through YouTube AI tutorials, LinkedIn, or referrals. They're skeptical of "AI agencies" because they've been burned by overpromising tech vendors before. They respond to directness, transparency, and proof — not buzzwords.

## Use Cases
1. Understand what Clear Automations actually does (in 10 seconds)
2. See the 3-phase offer and what each phase delivers
3. Compare their current broken process to what it looks like automated
4. See transparent pricing (most competitors hide it — we show it)
5. Request a free AI audit of their business

## Brand Personality
- **Direct** — says what it does, no jargon, no "leverage synergies"
- **Confident** — doesn't need to convince you AI is real. Shows you the system.
- **Trustworthy** — transparent pricing, "you own everything," no vendor lock-in
- **Competent** — enterprise experience (Big 4 consulting) applied to SMB problems
- **NOT:** flashy, startup-y, "disrupting," techy-for-the-sake-of-techy

## Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial industrial.** Think Bloomberg Businessweek meets a well-organized warehouse. Every section has purpose. No decoration for decoration's sake.

- **Palette:** Off-white/warm stone base (#F5F2ED or similar) with deep charcoal text (#1C1917) and a single warm accent (burnt amber #C2742F or terracotta). Navy (#1E293B) for contrast sections. NO neon, NO gradients, NO glowing anything.
- **Typography:** Slab or geometric display face for headlines (something with character — NOT Inter/Roboto). Clean humanist sans for body. The type does the heavy lifting.
- **Layout:** Left-aligned, generous margins, intentional asymmetry. Some sections full-bleed, others narrow. The page breathes unevenly — tight where urgency matters, spacious where trust-building happens.
- **Imagery:** Zero. No photos, no illustrations, no icons-in-circles. Typography + spacing + the occasional rule/line/geometric element. The content IS the design.
- **Motion:** Minimal. Maybe a subtle stagger on section entry. No parallax, no floating elements, no bouncing CTAs.

## What Makes This Unforgettable
The pricing is right there on the page. No "book a call to learn pricing." That alone makes this site stand out from every AI agency that hides behind "custom quotes." The before/after transformation table is visceral enough that SMB owners feel seen.

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do These)
- Dark mode with cyan/purple gradients (every AI agency)
- Robot/brain/circuit board illustrations
- "Powered by AI" badges
- Cards with icons above every heading
- Centered-everything layouts
- "Schedule a demo" as the only CTA with zero information about what you actually get
- Glassmorphism, blur effects, glow borders
- Stock photos of people pointing at screens
